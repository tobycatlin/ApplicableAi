iCat Solutions Ltd is a leading healthcare technology company focused on transforming the way medical facilities manage their patient care and medical workflows. We leverage cutting-edge technologies to provide innovative and secure solutions, which improve patient outcomes and streamline healthcare processes. 
We are looking for an experienced Backend Developer with expertise in the medical field to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in healthcare technology, including HL7 and DICOM protocol communications, and extensive knowledge of modern software development practices and technologies. 
Responsibilities: 
Design, develop, and maintain scalable and efficient backend and frontend services for our healthcare platform 
Implement data exchange and communication protocols, such as HL7 and DICOM, to support seamless integration with various medical systems and devices 
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features 
Ensure the performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications through continuous testing and monitoring 
Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback to peers 
Assist with DevOps tasks, including deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of our applications and infrastructure 
Contribute to the continuous improvement of our development processes and practices 
Requirements: 
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field 
3+ years of experience in backend software development 
Strong understanding of healthcare technology, including HL7 and DICOM protocol communications 
Proficient in .NET, NodeJS, and relational databases 
Familiarity CI/CD tools 
Experience with Git and version control best practices 
Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and communication skills 
Ability to work independently and as part of a team 
Love Apple products!! 
Nice-to-haves: 
Experience with containerization technologies, such as Docker and Kubernetes 
Familiarity with cloud platforms, such as AWS 
Understanding of medical terminology and regulatory requirements (e.g., HIPAA) 
Previous experience working in a healthcare or medical-related organization 
What We Offer: 
Competitive salary and benefits package 
Flexible work arrangements 
Opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies and contribute to meaningful projects in the healthcare industry 
Professional development and growth opportunities 
A collaborative, supportive, and inclusive work environment 
To Apply: 
Please submit your resume and cover letter, detailing your experience and qualifications, to info@icat.solutions. We look forward to reviewing your application and discussing how you can contribute to the success of iCat Solutions.
