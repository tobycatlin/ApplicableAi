Cambridgeshire office location but remote offered 
Our global client is looking for a BI Data Analyst to contribute to the design and development of BI reports for the business. They are undergoing a period of exciting expansion and are seeking a proactive and motivated individual to join their Data Systems team and become the SME for the new reporting platform, SAP Analytics Cloud. 
Responsibilities: 
– Collaborating with the Head of Business Systems and key stakeholders. 
– Creating cross-functional reports by gathering and analysing data from financial, operational, CRM/marketing, and other sources. 
– Developing self-service dashboards and scheduled reporting solutions using SAP Analytics Cloud. 
– Ensuring the accuracy of all reporting, maintaining a robust data environment. 
– Providing training and support to colleagues on accessing and interpreting published solutions. 
Requirements: 
– 4-5 years experience using BI solutions such as Power BI, Tableau, SAP Analytics Cloud etc, essential 
– In-depth finance understanding of financial processes and reporting needs, desirable 
– Strong knowledge of user experience design, data visualization techniques, and best practices. 
– Excellent understanding of business processes and accounting concepts. 
– Familiarity with data warehousing and ETL concepts. 
– Knowledge of an ERP solution and system administration are advantageous
