ANDRITZ Feed and Biofuel is one of the world’s leading suppliers of technology and services for the animal feed, aqua feed, pet food and biofuel industries. 
We offer an extensive line of equipment for the production of high-quality feed and biomass products. We pair our equipment with our state of the art automation solutions to ensure full process traceability. 
We have decades of experience designing and building feed and biomass plants including engineering, installation, start-up and commissioning, as well as replacement parts and service. This experience has resulted in hundreds of successful feed and biomass plants all over the world. 
Job description 
Location 
Ideally located in the North of England working remotely across the UK and Ireland reporting to Andritz Feed and Biofuel, Hull, UK, the position will join our team of Engineers. Majority of the time working at customer sites mainly in the UK but also internationally if required. 
Reporting to 
Service Coordinator, UK. 
Essential 
Mechanical education with experience in servicing and mounting of mechanical drive components including gearboxes and bearings. 
Knowledge of basic Electronics and Hydraulic is an advantage. 
Must be used to working in a demanding heavy industrial environment 
Responsibilities 
You will be part of a well- established team of Service Engineers in the UK and other European countries supporting our customers mainly in the UK. 
You will have the opportunity to become part of an exciting business where you are challenged both professionally and personally. You will work with competent and highly qualified colleagues from many departments both nationally and internationally. The company is in growth, we have experienced increased activity both nationally and internationally which gives our Employees good opportunities for development in all areas. 
Qualifications & Experience 
You have experience from a similar job ideally from a Feed Mill or Biofuel / Waste Processing background: 
Producing and completing Proactive Preventative Maintenance programs for the equipment 
Good knowledge and experience of performing lifting and slinging tasks 
Practical Mechanical experience 
Service minded and customer centric 
Flexible on working hours 
Willing to travel and have driver’s License B 
Familiar with MS Office tools 
Personal Characteristics 
Able to work independently but also a team player 
Have a focus on quality 
Service oriented and seek solutions 
Flexible, cooperative and punctual 
The position is full time. 
Other benefits include:- 
Salary range £40,000 - £45,000 plus overtime dependent on experience. 
Up to 8% Employer pension, 25 days annual leave, optional health cover & cycle to work scheme, Employee discount & voucher scheme.
