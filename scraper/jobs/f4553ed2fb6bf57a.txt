The position we are recruiting for is a Salesforce Developer, helping us implement Salesforce from scratch and the ground up. The successful person will have a number of years experience developing Salesforce into other companies and will help us achieve a great partnership with Salesforce. Ideally this person will know best practices, some challenges which may arise and help guide our team through the integration process. 
Responsibilities: 
Support business requirements and deal with all the CRM needs of the business. 
Provide customised solutions using the Salesforce platform. 
To communicate with different project managers, stakeholders, and technicians and ensure efficient participation in all the different phases of development from testing to maintenance. 
To troubleshoot any bugs in the system. 
Create implementation timelines and development goals. 
Ensure the implementation of agreed architecture and infrastructure meets the scope of Solution Design phase. 
Monitor systems and infrastructure to ensure they meet both user needs and business goals at present and with the future in mind. 
Providing training to other junior developers to upskill and share Salesforce knowledge. 
Candidate requirements: 
Previous experience as a Salesforce Developer 
Salesforce Platform Developer 1 Certifications 
Salesforce Platform App Builder Certification 
Strong understanding of the Salesforce IDE (Integrated Development Environment) 
Salesforce Advanced Admin Certification 
Proficiency in various database scripting languages like MYSQL, Apex, Native, VisualForce 
Intermediate knowledge of JavaScript and C# language 
Intermediate knowledge of APEX coding standards 
Well-versed with the Lightning framework 
Hands-on experience with different types of tools of Salesforce IDE
