Main Purpose of the job: 
To identify, develop and deliver new and existing business opportunities within the Midlands region which will help the company achieve its sales target plus its business strategic objectives. 
To promote the Watts Company and the Watts brand by working with the customers in the supply chain. 
Field based role to head up and take ownership, develop, and increase growth for the Water Utility, Water Utility OEM business and Automation business with valve and actuators. Identify and develop new and existing business opportunities with the customers in the region. Work with the Valves companies, Consultants, Specifiers, Contractors, Installers and Commissioning Engineers to provide technical support on the Watts and Socla product ranges. Support the External and Internal Sales Team on the Watts and Socla products. 
To promote the Watts Company and the Watts product brand by working with the customers within the supply chain. 
Responsibilities and Duties 
The Regional Sales Manager (RSM) for the Midlands will be responsible for the development of the region including turnover and margin. 
The RSM will take ownership of all business within the Midlands region. 
The RSM will be responsible for producing and implementing a robust and sustainable business strategy to demonstrate year on year growth to meet the company’s aims, goals, and objectives. 
Identify key influencers within the customers and region at all level to develop business strategies and make Watts a tier one or a preferred supplier. 
Setting, negotiating, and implementing all agreed teams and conditions (T&C’s) with the supply chain within the customer and Watts. 
Identify new business opportunities within the region and develop into orders. 
Work and support the Watts Internal and External Sales Team to develop business through the Merchant supply chain. 
Achieve and exceed sales targets and margins set by Watts and the UK Sales Leader for the Midlands. 
Develop, implement processes and systems to improve the efficiency within Watts and to meet the customers KPI’s and for continuous improvement plus quality. 
Working, liaising, and communicating with all other departments within Watts to develop and drive the business growth for the region and markets within Watts. 
Assist in the development of sales and marketing literature and associated documents to improve the Watts brand awareness and recognition plus point of sales through the Merchant branches and trade counters. 
Provide the main source of expertise to the company on business development issues in relation to the Midlands region and the market sectors. 
Update the company CRM system with all information on the sales region which includes customers including orders and projects. 
Maintain all required records of sales and other relevant information to enable performance to be measured, monitored, qualified, and quantified. 
Support the Sales Leader within Watts Head Office to report performance, business, business growth, aims, goals and objectives and other reports back to the shareholder and the business. 
To follow the Company’s and Group’s internal and external procedures 
To carry out any such other duties that may reasonably be requested of you. 
Education 
Degree in Business or similar qualification. 
Management 
This role has no direct reports. 
Experience, skills, and qualities 
At least three years’ experience in solution sales working with Water Utility Companies, Merchants, Wholesalers, Distributors, Consultants, Developers, OEM’s, Valves Companies and End Users with a proven sales track record. 
Excellent Commercial knowledge and experience. 
Good communication and presentation skills. 
An extensive understanding of the industry and the customers within the industry. 
Excellent capability of Word, Excel, Power Point, CRM systems (Salesforce) 
Outlook and other computer programs plus a strong understand of 
spreadsheets.
