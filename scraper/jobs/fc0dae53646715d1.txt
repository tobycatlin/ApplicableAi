Speechmatics is a world-leading expert in speech technology. Our mission has always been to "Understand Every Voice," meaning that we aim to make voice technology inclusive and accessible to everyone in the world, regardless of demographic, age, gender, accent, dialect, or location. This is an ambitious goal, and to achieve it, we're continuously innovating and experimenting, all while providing value to our diverse range of current customers. 
Speechmatics are seeking an Infrastructure Engineer to tackle technical challenges related to datacenter HPC hardware, Layer 2/3 networking, and Linux OS automation. As part of a team, you'll help design, implement, and enhance the infrastructure and systems supporting our company. You will collaborate with other teams to improve reliability and performance of internal services and machine learnings workloads. 
Your opportunity: 
Be an integral part of the team! We're a tight-knit group, and the tech we own is interconnected with many other teams, so you'll have a huge opportunity to positively influence efficiency of our business and overall product delivery. 
You're pragmatic, and able to balance "doing things right" with "doing things quickly," depending on the context. You can navigate short-term changes in priorities as we work towards longer-term goals. 
You're passionate about creating high quality solutions and enjoy the exploratory process of improving performance, stability, and maintainability of the tech you work with, no matter where a problem lies. 
You feel comfortable developing software, and you're interested or experienced in working with some of the technologies we use: Python, Ansible, Docker, Kubernetes, Grafana, Prometheus, Gitlab, developing in a Linux environment. 
Responsibilities: 
Build, configure, and deploy new compute, network, storage, and specialist security infrastructure, ensuring correct configuration and up to date with latest patches. 
Resolve escalations and issues, improve the internal customer experience, and drive initiatives that increase success of other teams' projects. 
Assisting with migration of VMware infrastructure to Kubernetes. 
Implement new cost-efficient solutions according to budgets, develop capacity plans to optimise existing and future usage of internal resources. 
Examine, refine, and develop the existing monitoring and metrics of the company's internal infrastructure. 
Coordinate comprehensive disaster recovery architecture and operations
