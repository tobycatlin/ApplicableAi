Our Company 
Anglia Electrical and Security Limited have been powering and protecting the Eastern Region since 2002. We are continuing to expand quickly and are fast-growing across all our disciplines offered and therefore need to recruit a hard-working, dedicated engineer to join our team. As a BAFE and SSAIB certified company, we offer professional and quality workmanship. Although we are a Suffolk based company we do cover, Suffolk, Norfolk and Essex providing design, installation and maintenance throughout the Domestic, Industrial and commercial markets. Ideal candidates need to be based with within Suffolk or Norfolk.

Job Description 
We are looking for an energetic, knowledgeable and confident Security Engineer to join our busy team on a field based role. Our impressive growth rate is due to our excellent reputation and trusted expertise in our field and we wish to continue on this trajectory. The ideal candidate will have experience and competence in the installation, and maintenance of security systems. Although it would be beneficial to have experience across multiple sectors, this is not essential if you can provide specialist knowledge in a particular area. 
Responsibilities 
Install and maintain security protection systems 
Perform site visits to ensure compliance with required permits and standards 
Work to a high Health and Safety standard 
Proactively work independently to achieve all assigned daily jobs 
Provide technical support to our customers 
Work alongside your fellow team members on projects 
Effective communication between colleagues and administration team 
Provide quality control on all aspects of jobs undertaken 
Experience/Requirements 
Minimum of 3 years’ experience in the security industry including installation and servicing 
Due to the nature of our business, all successful applicants will be subject to a satisfactory Security Screening and DBS check 
A full, clean and valid driver’s license is required 
IPAF, ECS, CSCS Cards would also be beneficial 
A positive attitude and the ability to self-motivate is a must 
Proactive approach on working with our team within the business
