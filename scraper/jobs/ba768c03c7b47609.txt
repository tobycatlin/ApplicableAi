Our UX team is responsible for making the experience of using our platform simple, intuitive and delightful for all of our customers. We do that by helping each other deliver consistently outstanding work. We’re confident in our platform, market potential and we have big plans for the future which is why we are looking for another UX designer to join the team. 
What’s the opportunity for a UX Designer at Gearset? 
Join our growing and experienced UX team, laser-focused on delighting our users, and always ready and available to support you 
You’ll own & drive the full design process from discovery through to delivery: turning research insights into ideas, designing the workflows and UI for features and getting them shipped in product releases 
Partner with product managers to research and understand the jobs to be done for our customers 
Work closely with engineering teams to ship projects that help address customer needs 
This role comes with plenty of room for growth and you’ll have ownership of design decisions in your work 
What you’ll achieve 
Working with customers to understand their needs; you’ll plan & conduct generative product research with product managers 
Iterating on ideas, with peers, to solve those needs; you’ll share & seek feedback on work with other designers, product managers and engineers 
Prototyping solutions to validate their value with users; you’ll organise & lead evaluative research with engineering teams, focusing on target users 
Collaborating with teams to get the right solution shipped on our platform; you’ll pair with & help engineers get features into the hands of our customers 
Advocate and initiate new design and engineering work to improve shipped projects; you’ll champion consistency & quality of experience to raise the bar for quality at Gearset 
About you 
You are enthusiastic about working with customers to build and deliver the right solution that empowers them 
You can use Figma to create ideas for design work and are comfortable working with HTML & CSS to finesse the design details in code 
Love designing simple, intuitive and delightful user interfaces & care about design details 
Are pragmatic when it comes to design choices and make objective design decisions, always advocating for the best possible user experience 
Have experience shipping products and working in a continuous development environment 
Great to haves 
Experience working on technical applications or developer tools 
Have experience of working with hosted (SaaS) products

About us 
Gearset is the leading DevOps solutions for the millions of people developing on Salesforce, the world's biggest CRM. We’ve the fast paced exciting environment of a start-up, with the success, stability, and ambition of a scale up. 
In a few short years, we’ve rocketed from a 7 person start-up to over 200 people, and have become the DevOps solution of choice for some of the world’s biggest companies, including IBM, McKesson, and even Salesforce themselves. In the last 18 months we’ve opened offices in Belfast and Chicago, alongside our Cambridge (UK) HQ. 
We’re proud of what we’ve built and how we’ve built it. We work as a team, where attributes of trust, openness and honesty are key, as they allow us to have a feedback-driven culture that keeps us improving to deliver the best solution for our customers. 
Alongside our Glassdoor score of 4.2/5, and customer G2 scores of 4.7/5, we recently received three awards from Best Companies UK in 2022. This includes being a Top 5 Company to work for in the East of England, UK Top 5 Best Mid-Sized Company and Top 5 Best Technology Company to work for in the whole of the UK. We also won a special award for Learning & Development that reflects our ongoing commitment to the personal goals and ambitions of our employees.
