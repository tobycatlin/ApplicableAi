Are you ready to unleash your full potential? We’re looking for people who are passionate about payments to chart Worldpay’s path to being the largest and most-loved payments company in the world. 
About the team 
Access Worldpay is a talented, creative group working together to bring the next generation payment gateway. Our clients range from small/medium businesses and start-ups to some of the largest tech and digital firms. We use global collaboration to support global clients. 
Our group is a place where learning and growing are core parts of the journey. We care deeply about things like immutability, automation, test driven development and agile ways of working. More information on our principles is available in https://worldpay.github.io/gateway-tribe-principles and about our APIs in https://developer.worldpay.com/. You can also find more details about our approach and culture in https://developer.worldpay.com/newsroom. 
What you will be doing 
You will collaborate with your cross-functional squad to design, develop, operate and improve the products our customers enjoy using. You will work with the wider engineering team to bring solutions to challenges that require cross squad collaboration. As a member of the squad you will also have deep understanding of the product and industry you are building for. You will also have the opportunity to be a teacher for less experienced engineers. 
Main Duties and Responsibilities: 
Collaboratively develop the features and engineering capabilities for the products your squad own. We aim to employ automation for everything 
Collaborate in all stages of development from requirement discussions with the Product Owner to the deployment of the feature in the cloud 
Join the on-call rota to support the highly available products developed by the squad 
Provide your squad with the tools to enable data driven decision making 
Supporting and mentoring more junior engineers within the squad 
Collaborate with your fellow engineers from other squads to make decisions around technologies, patterns and approaches to adopt 
What you bring: 
Experience designing, implementing and operating large scale and highly available microservices deployed to the cloud 
Experience building microservices with Docker, Scala and Reactive Programming. 
Experience with Test Driven Development 
An engineering skillset that is focused on quality and a test first philosophy, regardless of the activity 
An agile mindset where you will drive both your squad and the wider tribe towards better ways of working 
An inquisitive personality that is always looking for constant improvement opportunity 
Added bonus if you have: 
Experience with Play Framework and Akka. 
Experience with Kafka or any other messaging oriented middleware 
Experience with reactive programming 
Exposure to the Payments industry domain (especially Cards and other payment schemes) 
Exposure to PCI-DSS 
What we offer you 
A competitive salary and benefits 
A variety of career development tools, resources and opportunities 
The chance to work on some of the most challenging, relevant issues in the payment industry 
Time to support charities and give back in your community
