Software / Program Developer - Remote 
We are urgently seeking an Lead Microsoft Applications Developer help support and deliver a number of solutions to our customers using the Microsoft stack of Technologies. 
This for our client who are a Global IT Service Provider. This will be on a contract and based remotely. 
Your role We are looking for a Lead Microsoft Applications developer to help support and deliver a number of solutions to our customers using the Microsoft stack of Technologies. The role will involve working on well-established System Integration projects delivering mission critical Enterprise solutions to our customers. You will be joining an experienced team and helping to deliver key change controls within the account to delivering both innovation and business value. You will have the opportunity to contribute to the next generation cloud ready integration framework which is currently being developed inhouse by the Integration team. The role will provide an excellent environment for learning new technologies and working with skilled resources and growing your career. 
This role will also include a level of business analysis responsibilities whereby the individual will work closely with the customer to understand their requirements clearly in order to determine how these impact on existing systems with the ability to scope change both from a technical and cost aspect
