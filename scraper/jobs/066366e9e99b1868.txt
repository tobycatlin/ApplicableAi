At Mundipharma, we are proud of the work we do to bring innovative treatments to patients. We challenge ourselves constantly to deliver more for patients, healthcare professionals, our partners, and our employees. 
The Team 
The Inventory Data Analyst role sits in the Central Supply Chain / Technical Operations team at Mundipharma. The role will be accountable for the Global inventory analytics and proactively managing key results by managing and tracking around $150M of inventory, reporting of related KPIs, providing variance analysis and actively delivering inventory balancing initiatives. 
Role and responsibilities 
Reporting and close review of the global inventory results 
Reporting of related KPI’s and providing variance analysis 
Support with the partnering with the functions to report the inventory budget and monthly rolling projection 
Interpret inventory management financial results to management including variances and the recommendations 
Supporting Inventory related projects and their implementation, Support the identification of savings through Central Supply Chain 
In-depth statistical analysis using advanced techniques to support a wide variety of business initiatives such as customer segmentation or behavioural models 
Proactively mines data warehouses to identify trends and patterns and generates insights for business units and senior leadership 
Creates interactive dashboards, reports, and presentations to effectively communicate complex analytical findings to stakeholders 
Ensure compliance with statutory duties, regulation and legislation. Ensure activities meet with and integrate with organisational requirements for Quality management, health and safety, legal, Code of Business Ethics, environmental policies and general duty of care. 
Ensure compliance with GxP related activities. 
What you’ll bring 
Experience with descriptive analytics, statistical analysis, modelling segmentation, and data mining using large databases 
Proficiency in data visualisation tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or Looker Studio 
Proficiency in data science and analytics tools/platforms 
Ability to synthesise and clearly communicate large volumes of complex information to senior management of various technical understandings 
Excellent organisation, multitasking, prioritisation, and deadline management skills 
Hands-on knowledge of statistical methods and techniques such as predictive modelling, segmentation, and regression 
Ability to collaborate and excel in complex, cross-functional teams involving data scientists, business analysts, and stakeholders 
Mathematical/statistical abilities to perform data gathering, quantitative and qualitative data analysis 
End to End Supply Chain experience would be advantageous 
Previous experience in the Pharmaceutical industry would be advantageous

Mundipharma is dedicated to bringing innovative treatments to patients in the areas of Pain Management, Infectious Disease and Consumer Healthcare as well as other severe debilitating disease areas. Our guiding principles, centred around Integrity and Patients-Centricity, are at the heart of everything we do.
