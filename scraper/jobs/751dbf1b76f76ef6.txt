Dynamics 365 CE Developer, Bishops Stortford, £55,000 + Bonus, 3+ years of project work, Hybrid role - 4 days wfh, 1 day in the office per week, D365 CE & Power Platform Project work, Dedicated inhouse development team 
A longstanding law firm headquartered in Bishops Stortford are looking for a D365 CE Developer to join their inhouse development team. You will work alongside several other developers who sit as part of a larger 15 person IT team. This is a hybrid role with 4 days working from home and 1 day in the Bishop Stortford office per week. 
They have been running Dynamics CRM for multiple years, recently upgrading to D365 CE online and looking to roll out D365 to other areas of the business. The wider IT team has business analysts who will handle the bulk of the stakeholder engagement, requirements gathering etc, resulting in this role being a focussed development role. You will utilise out of the box customisations, C# plugin development, PowerApps and Power Automate in your solutions. They have a healthy project pipeline of over 3 years of project work signed off. 
The requirements are… 
Strong knowledge of D365 CE & Power Platform 
Skilled in customisation and configuration 
Technical skillset including C# and JavaScript 
Experience writing custom C# plugins 
Full software development lifecycle experience 
Power BI knowledge is advantageous 
Strong teamworking ability 
If you're interested in this role or to discuss other Dynamics CRM/365 CE opportunities - send your CV to Matt Lynn at m.lynn@nigelfrank.com or you can call me directly on 0191 814 7980.
