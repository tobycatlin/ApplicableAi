Simpson Technology Ltd are a sales and service company supplying sheet metal processing machinery and are based in Heald Green/Cheadle. We are a high energy and hard working team of professionals and are passionate about our people. Due to our recent expansion we are looking for a very enthusiastic individual to join us in an engineer's role who is based in Lancashire/Derbyshire/Yorkshire/Midlands area to cover customers in the UK and Ireland. 
ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES 
Installation of CNC lasers, bending and punching machines, software and automation 
Schedule service check overs and organize service consumables with Admin 
Provide preventative breakdown maintenance to our contract customers 
Troubleshoot over the telephone and on-site 
Provide training for operators, maintenance and programmers 
Assist Sales with technical support 
Manage phone calls and correspondence 
Responsible for the completion and submission of field service reports and expense reports 
Work closely with our suppliers for the customers benefit 
Ability to generate additional revenue for the company through a bonus scheme 
Other responsibilities may be requested to assist within the team. 
QUALIFICATION AND EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS 
Time served in engineering with at least an electrical/electronic qualification but preferably multiskilled 
3 years of work experience with CNC machines 
PREFERRED SKILLS 
Experience in CNC sheet metal machines preferred but not essential 
Ability to interpret electrical schematics 
High customer orientation 
Excellent verbal and written communication skills 
Good knowledge of MS Office (Outlook, Word, Excel) & CRM systems (preferably Salesforce) 
Excellent electrical/electronic troubleshooting skills 
Pneumatic and hydraulic experience advantageous 
Ability to stay calm under pressure 
Ability to work independently and make decisions 
Work within a small team 
Travel on short notice may be required. Available to work weekends and after normal work hours as required which will be paid at an overtime rate 
Benefits: 
On-site parking

Company events & social hours
