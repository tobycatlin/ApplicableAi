About Us: 
Homemove is a fast-paced Proptech startup revolutionising the way people move home. As a dynamic and innovative team, we are constantly looking for ways to improve and streamline the home moving process through the use of cutting-edge technology. We're seeking a talented and motivated Full Stack Developer to join our collaborative team and help us take our solutions to the next level. 
Job Description: 
The successful candidate will be responsible for building and maintaining web applications using a variety of modern technologies. This includes but is not limited to: Laravel, Livewire, Tailwind CSS, Alpine.js. You will be working on all aspects of our products from database to UI, and everything in between. 
Responsibilities: 
Develop and maintain our web apps using Laravel, Livewire, Alpine.js as required 
Collaborate with team to design and launch new features 
Maintain code integrity and organisation 
Understanding and implementation of security and data protection 
Requirements: 
Solid experience with PHP and Laravel is required 
Experience with Laravel Livewire, Alpine.js, or jQuery is a strong plus 
Proficiency with modern development tools and practices (TDD, Git, CI/CD, etc.) 
Experience with Forge, Docker, MySQL, REST and Microservices 
Familiarity with NodeJS, VueJS, NextJS is a plus 
Excellent communication and collaboration skills 
Able to mentor junior developers and lead projects when necessary 
Able to manage technical debt and refactor code 
Willingness to learn new technologies and programming languages 
At least 8 years of commercial experience in web development 
Education: 
Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or similar field preferred, but not required if the candidate can demonstrate equivalent practical experience 
Benefits: 
Competitive salary and benefits package 
A supportive and friendly work environment 
Opportunities for professional development and advancement 
Equity options in the company 
Flexible working hours 
Interested applicants should submit a CV and a brief cover letter outlining their suitability for the role. We look forward to hearing from you.
