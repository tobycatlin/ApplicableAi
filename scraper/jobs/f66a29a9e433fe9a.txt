About Arcus 
Arcus is a fast growing and successful GovTech company. We are dynamic and forward thinking, with great ambition. At Arcus, you will have the chance to share your knowledge and make your presence felt. With our people at the core of everything we do, we don’t want people to ‘fit in’, diversity and difference are essential to creativity. We want people to thrive in our open and collaborative environment and be an integral part of our close knit team. 
So, if you are looking for something more than ‘just a job’, you are in the right place. We have big company benefits, including Private Medical Insurance, Life Assurance and Pension, as well as Volunteering Days, Health & Wellbeing benefits and social activities. 
If this all appeals and you have the ambition to match ours, then read on! 
The Job Role 
This is a technical role, working within the Implementation Serviced Department as a specialist resource where you will be managing the migration and deployment of customer data and configuration as well as setting up scheduled data export/import routines. 
Responsibilities: 
Helping customers understand their legacy and target data models and recommending actions to assist in effective migration 
Analysis of customer data and production of data quality reports outlining data quality issues and recommending “quick wins” for fix and highlighting issues 
Accurate Data mapping between legacy and new schemas 
Test and final load of data sets into customer environments in such a way as to give confidence in completeness and accuracy, minimising business impact 
Working directly with customers to support them in getting the most out of Salesforce.com and feeding back to product teams where data model adjustments would be beneficial 
Managing your own time whilst working within the project constraints 
Taking part in project planning exercises and ensuring data concerns are given full consideration 
Setup of inbound/outbound data integrations and assisting with any related support tickets 
Preparation of data mapping templates, data containers and ongoing development of supporting documentation 
Is this You? 
Requirements: 
A deep interest in, and enjoyment of working with data is essential for this role 
Experience using your choice of tooling (MS Excel, MS Access, SQL, Jitterbit, Alteryx, Open Refine, Informatica, Talend) to extract, transform and load data between systems 
You should expect to be working across several projects simultaneously and be comfortable working to customer deadlines 
Ability to quickly adapt to new situations, different business practices and challenges of working in the public sector 
A proven ability to plan, organise, prioritise, manage and track numerous concurrent activities to successful outcomes 
Business Intelligence & reporting experience - Both the process of developing and displaying performance indicators and tooling e.g. Tableau, Silk, Qlik or the BI tools within AWS such as QuickSight (desirable) 
Experience with data modelling - A working knowledge (or beyond) of data modelling techniques would be a distinct advantage. For example being able to interpret, write and (to the right audience) explain an ERD would help you hit the ground running in this role
