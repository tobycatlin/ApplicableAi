About Fleet Assist 
Fleet Assist provides network management services to 38 of the UK's leading leasing & contract hire companies with a combined fleet size exceeding 1.1m vehicles and annual maintenance spend exceeding £200m. 
Fleet Assist is an automotive supply chain management services specialist which has multiple products tailored specifically to the leasing, contract hire and short-term rental industries with its core SMR Services supported by the Atlas award-winning intelligent platform and database. 
The Fleet Assist name has become synonymous with market leading services, securing preferential rates, service levels and terms across multiple verticals. Network Supply Chain Management At the core of the Fleet Assist proposition is its network of over 4,800 garages comprising Franchised, Fast fit and Independents through which preferential terms and conditions are in place. Its award-winning system “Atlas” system is utilised across the network delivering significant efficiencies to leasing companies and garages. The net result is improved purchasing terms for leasing companies, improved service level management and business intelligence that provides market leasing visibility enabling improved SMR spend management. Service Booking 
The in-house team delivers a personalised service – booking customers’ vehicles in for SMR jobs nationally on their behalf. Atlas is designed to cater for an infinite combination of customer and vehicle specific policies matched to suitable garages, booking the vehicle into the most appropriate garage based on cost, distance or any other work direction criteria for the work required. Its own system delivers highly effective work booking facilities to the network through an experienced in-house team. Fleet Assist’s experienced and expert Technical Authorisation team help customers control maintenance expense for vehicle fleets. The team has a library of industry data that enables it to make the correct decisions in a timely fashion.
