AD Group Services Ltd are seeking experienced Door Entry Engineers to join our well established team. Our client base is mainly for the social housing sector providing security solutions to housing estates across London. 
You will be provided with a company vehicle, all equipment (except hand tools), mobile phone and uniform.Location of work will be London area and the Home Counties with our office based in Royston. 
AD Group Services Ltd provide a range of services such as CCTV, Door Entry, Access Control, Door & Gate Automation Systems to commercial clients and social landlords. 
We also have a fabrication division which designs and builds gates, railings, stair cases and almost anything from metal. 
We offer 28 days holiday (inclusive of bank holidays) 
Responsibilities and Duties 
Installation, Maintainence & Servicing of 
Door entry systems from 1 way up to multi-way, multi-block, multi door 
Access control systems from 1 door to multiple, remotely controlled, IP, 4G and GSM 
CCTV systems from Analogue to the latest Digital IP Technologies 
Gate Automation (if you want to learn this side of the business) 
You may also be required to undertake Planned Maintenance / service visits for Door Entry, Access Control and CCTV systems where demand is high. 
Qualifications and Skills 
Communication skills (written and verbal) 
Customer Service skills 
Good Fault / Problem solving skills 
Flexible and adaptable 
Good organisational skills 
Experience of using test equipment 
Self-motivated with the willingness to learn 
Be able to think "outside the box" on some occasions
