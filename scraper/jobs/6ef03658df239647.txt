Are you a QA Engineer with 2 years' experience working on a commercial web application? Have you got experience of developing and expanding automation regression packs to cut down manual regression time? 
Do you have experience of API and performance testing? 
If this sounds like you, and a salary of up to £35,000 and remote working (within the UK) appeals to you, then let's chat! Worth noting - they do meet ONCE per quarter in Cambridge. 
So, what about the company? 
In short, they help ensure that patients get the medical help they need by reduce waiting times and providing outpatient appointments online. They're growing again and as a result want to hire another QA engineer to their team. We have placed the majority of the QA team into this company and everyone has said positive things about their journey with the business so far! 
You will be joining a small team of manual and automation testers. 
Expand automation regression packs to cut down manual regression time as well as carrying out Functional, API and Perfomance testing 
Cross-browser testing, increasing coverage and efficiency 
Adding tests to manual regression suite, ensuring it's consistent with latest system capabilities and can be executed efficiently with minimum supervision 
Hands-on experience of migrating manual tests to an automated framework 
Developing test cases written in Gherkin format for acceptance criteria of stories 
Participation in stand-ups and and sprint planning, helping estimate QA timescales
