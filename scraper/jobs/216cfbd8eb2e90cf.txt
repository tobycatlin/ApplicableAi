Artlist is a leading Creative Technology company - its where the creative arts meet technology. If there’s one thing we all have in common, it’s a love of music and film, which is why we build innovative products to help content creators make amazing videos. 
We do this by giving them the best music, footage, sound effects, and editing software around. We also revolutionised the industry with a radical new music licensing model that has since become the global standard, offering a subscription-based licence that covers every video project. 
Artlist is now the go-to platform for over 16M content creators across more than 160 countries. Our users range from beginner social creators to superbrands like Apple, Google, Nike, Coca-Cola, and Netflix. 
The role 
We're looking for a Full-Stack Developer to join our amazing UK R&D Team. 
The stack that you'll be working with is across React/ Node.js/ Typescript and you will support the team developing both front-end and back-end systems. 
As a Full-Stack Developer at Artlist, you'll further harness your knowledge of software development to help build next generation web applications and take our products to the next level. You’ll be part of a talented team based here in Norwich that is part of a wider team globally. 
Requirements: 
Required skills 
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or related field (or equivalent work experience). 
Demonstrable experience with front-end technologies, including React and TypeScript. 
Experience with Node.js 
Experience with RESTful API design and implementation. 
Familiarity with database systems such as PostgreSQL, MySQL. 
Interested in working as part of a vibrant and sociable team. 
A fast learner, able to quickly master concepts, disciplines and methods. 
The ability to articulate their thoughts and challenges with their peers. 
Desirable skills 
Frameworks such as Next.js/NestJS 
Experience with TDD, BDD 
Knowledge of domain driven development 
Familiar with cloud-based platforms (AWS, Azure Devops, Google Cloud etc). 
Experience with build and deployment tools such as Gitlab pipelines, Jenkins. 
Experience with Docker and Kubernetes 
Wake up for this 
Work alongside and learn from passionate, industry leading developers that are keen to continually develop their skills. 
Write highly scalable services in order to serve many customers. 
Design and build distributed systems with event driven architecture. 
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and implement new features for artists that host their content on Artlist. 
Take full ownership and accountability of projects. 
Develop a range of genuinely exciting products in a creative industry.
