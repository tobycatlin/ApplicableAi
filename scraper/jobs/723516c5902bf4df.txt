We are a digital marketing technology and services provider serving the financial services industry and we're looking for full stack .NET Developers to join our diverse team. 
As a member of the team, you’ll be personally involved in designing, maintaining and coding features and will work closely with colleagues across the company to deliver the vision of secure, scalable, engaging and intuitive experiences for our clients. 
This is a remote role with occasional days in our Stevenage office. 
Essential: 
3+ years’ experience 
NET MVC 
C# or VB.NET 
Microsoft SQL Server 
Modern web standards (HTML5, CSS3) and techniques (responsive) 
Strong understanding of the principles of Object Oriented Programming 
Strong problem-solving skills and an ability to work through problems 
A desire to create clean reusable code 
Beneficial: 
Entity Framework, or other Object Relational Mapping framework; 
JavaScript 
.Net Core 
Azure DevOps 
Defensive coding 
An ability to write code that scales 
Benefits 
Competitive salary dependant on experience. 
Comprehensive benefits package.

Opportunity to work with a talented team on innovative developers.
